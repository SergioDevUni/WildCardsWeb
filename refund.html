Refund Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-29

WildCards sells digital items delivered instantly for use inside the application.

General policy

Because digital items are delivered immediately, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. If you experience a problem with delivery or functionality, contact support and we will help.

When a refund may be granted

  • You were charged but did not receive the digital item.
  • A technical issue prevented the item from being usable as described.
  • Unauthorized payment (we may request verification details).

Chargebacks and disputes

If a payment is disputed or charged back, we may revoke associated digital items or access to prevent fraud and loss.

How to request support

Contact us with your purchase details (date, amount, and any receipt reference) so we can investigate quickly.
